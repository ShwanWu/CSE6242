Average node degree(3.64): 
Each node has its degree which equals to lines connect to it. Average degree means the average value of all nodes' degree

Diameter of the graph(5): 
The greatest distance between pair of vertices. 

Avg. path length(2.784): 
Compute sum of path length between all the pairs of nodes and divided by sum of nodes.

Graph Density (0.037): 
The density of the objects that filling the graph. High density means centralized graph.
